About me

Childhood

I was born on the 22nd day of June mid 90s to parents who were high school teachers. Owing to the fact that they were both English teachers my childhood was spent mastering the language and surpassing the limits my older brother had set through poetry became one of my life goals. Afterstealing his first ever poetry book I grew to love this form of expression but had yet to realise the implications the art brought along with itself. It demanded that pain be felt and passions chased to the ends of the earth.
